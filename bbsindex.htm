Note:
  • "Flip Side" ( 裏 ) is a literal translation and means something like "perverted" but can also be understood as "weird", "random" or "other". It's basically used to indicate boards where pictures get uploaded that normally would not or should not be uploaded to their main counterparts.
  • "NETA" ( ネタ ) also has more than one kind of meaning. It can mean "meme", but also "spoof", news (or gag) material, stories you make up or any sorts of funny photoshops.
